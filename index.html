Where are our
energy form?

That solar farm

Customers can trace the source of their energy 24/7 which helps: Procure the right mix of renewables to match generation to demand Give confidence in their ability to take direct action Change behavior at scale shifting load and driving renewable energy demand Potential gamification of clean energy use to drive even more impactful engagement

The wind farm

Customers can trace the source of their energy 24/7, eliminating greenwashing, giving confidence in their ability to take direct action

Our family, friends and community

Buy and sell energy with family, friends and the broader community. See the source of your energy imports (gold on Powertracer) If you have solar panels, you can see who you are exporting it to (green), and set your own price

Our solar sites

Customers can share their energy across sites in a virtual net metering solution: Counting Scope 2 reductions at the ‘buying’ sites Encourages renewable energy use at all sites

No double trading

Powertracer shows you an auditable history of the source of every kWh you buy 24/7, ensuring energy cannot be sold twice.
